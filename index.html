Meet Tria

Born and raised in the heart of St. Louis, my love for baking began in my childhood kitchen. As a breast cancer survivor, I’ve learned that life is meant to be celebrated, and there’s no better way to do that than with a delicious treat. Every mini loaf I bake is a labor of love, crafted with the finest ingredients and a whole lot of heart. Thank you for supporting my passion!
